About This QR Solution Platform

This platform is designed to be a complete, browser-based QR management system. You can generate standard QR codes, branded codes with your own logo, restaurant / shop codes with table and menu details, and even bulk QR codes using text or CSV input.

The built-in QR scanner lets you read codes directly using your device camera, so you don’t have to install a separate app. All QR codes generated on this device are stored in local history, and when you log in with your account, they can also be backed up securely to the cloud using Firebase.

Typical use cases include business cards, product packaging, event tickets, restaurant menus, college projects, quick links to forms, notes or portfolios, and many more. Because everything runs on web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), it’s easy to deploy this platform on any hosting service or integrate it into a larger system.

The goal is to provide a flexible tool that is simple enough for students and small businesses, but powerful enough to be extended into a full SaaS product with analytics, role-based access, and advanced templates.
